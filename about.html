shubham kumar rishu, a fitness influencer and social media sensation from bihar,patna was featured in a video shared by Prime Minister Narendra Modi for the 'Swachhata Hi Seva' campaign. He had created a sensation on social media by completing the 75-Day hard challenge to imbibe the fitness culture among the youth.

He made five rules in his 75-day challenge on social media. The first rule was to do two workouts of 45 minutes in the morning and evening; the second rule was to follow the schedule strictly; the third rule was to drink four litres of water daily; the fourth rule was to take a selfie every day after the workout and the fifth rule was to read any non-fiction book for mental peace.
